♥ POETRY COLLECTION ♥

This is my poetry collection where I share the poems that I wrote!
I love reading and writing poems because I think it is one of the art forms that help me speak my emotions that I often couldn't seem to find the right words for them.


We could have been more than we thought.
I am nothing but a miserable town.
There is a better place. I just have to get there.
So far so good.
I am done being your fool.
I am my own superhero.
We are onto better things.
Waiting for better days.
Learning to make the most out of where I am standing now.
